Education

In the autumn of 2020, I enrolled at Karl-Franzens University Graz to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Physics. Additionally, I also enrolled at Graz University of Technology to complement my studies by pursuing a Bachelor's degree in SEM.

  • Physics BSc
  • Software Engineering and Management (SEM) BSc

During my undergraduate studies, I embarked on a journey to understand the complex dynamics of cellular automata, specifically focusing on modeling densest circle packing in two dimensions. This exploration resulted in fascinating observations, including the formation of intriguing hexagonal structures and events resembling Rayleigh breakup scenarios. If you're interested in delving into this captivating world of mathematical modeling, feel free to explore my bachelor's thesis.
